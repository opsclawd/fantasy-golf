# Competitive Analysis: Commissioner-First Fantasy Golf MVP

## Threat Ranking

1. Splash Sports
2. RunYourPool
3. OfficeFootballPool
4. DraftKings Fantasy Golf
5. FanDuel Fantasy Sports
6. Fantrax
7. Fantasy National
8. Data Golf
9. OfficePoolStop
10. PoolHost

## Why They Rank Here

### 1. Splash Sports
Most direct threat. Combines commissioner workflows, golf formats, payments, mobile UX, and trust messaging.

### 2. RunYourPool
Strong legacy host for golf pools with broad format coverage and familiar commissioner controls.

### 3. OfficeFootballPool
Legacy private pool host with golf formats and long trust history.

### 4. DraftKings Fantasy Golf
Powerful paid-fantasy substitute that can reduce the need for commissioner-run pools entirely.

### 5. FanDuel Fantasy Sports
Large attention and habit competitor in paid fantasy behavior, though not golf-pool-first.

### 6. Fantrax
Best for advanced fantasy league users who want deep customization.

### 7. Fantasy National
Strong golf research/planner substitute for serious players.

### 8. Data Golf
Analytics-heavy substitute for advanced golf users.

### 9. OfficePoolStop
Low-cost private-pool substitute, but less golf-specific.

### 10. PoolHost
Legacy free pool option with longevity, but weak golf specialization.

## Competitive Takeaways
- The market is fragmented
- Mobile cash-play is the most direct modern threat
- Legacy hosts still matter because they own commissioner habits
- Advanced analytics tools are substitutes for power users, not core pool hosts

## Strategic Implication
The MVP should compete on clarity, trust, and low commissioner burden rather than feature breadth.
