# Commissioner-First Fantasy Golf Pool MVP Brief

## Executive Summary
This product is a commissioner-first fantasy golf pool for private groups. It helps one organizer launch a tournament pool, invite players, lock picks on time, and deliver trustworthy live standings without spreadsheet chaos or cash-play complexity.

The opportunity is real because the category is fragmented: legacy pool hosts are familiar but dated, cash-play platforms are powerful but heavier, and DIY spreadsheets are flexible but painful. The wedge is not breadth; it is trust, speed, and simplicity for the commissioner, with a lightweight mobile experience for everyone else.

## The Problem
Commissioners do the most work and carry the most risk. They set up the pool, explain the rules, chase players, manage deadlines, and absorb blame when scoring or access breaks. Players want to participate, but they drop off when login flows are clunky, scoring is unclear, or live updates feel untrustworthy.

Today’s options force an ugly tradeoff: simple but clunky legacy hosts, modern but compliance-heavy cash platforms, or manual work in spreadsheets. None feel purpose-built for a golf-first private group that wants to get running fast before the next tournament.

## The Solution
Build a narrow MVP around the core private-pool workflow:
- Create a pool and choose a contest format
- Share a join link instantly
- Let players submit golfer picks on mobile
- Lock entries at tee time
- Refresh scores on a predictable cadence
- Show live standings with clear freshness and lock-state indicators

The product should feel calm and obvious: commissioners should know exactly what is happening, and players should always know where they stand.

## Who It Serves
Primary user: the private pool commissioner running golf contests for friends, offices, clubs, or recurring groups.

Secondary users: engaged golf fans who want strategic play and live standings.

Tertiary users: casual invitees who need a simple, mobile-friendly way to join and check results.

## What Makes This Different
- Commissioner peace of mind is the product.
- Scoring trust is treated as a core feature, not backend polish.
- The experience is golf-native and private-group-first, not a generic fantasy wrapper.
- The MVP avoids regulated money handling until the core workflow proves itself.
- The system is designed for future mobile expansion without coupling the product to native complexity too early.

## Scope
In scope for MVP:
- Pool creation
- Tournament selection
- Invite link sharing
- Player pick submission
- Locking at deadline
- Score refresh and live leaderboard
- Basic commissioner controls
- Clear stale-data and lock-state messaging

Out of scope for MVP:
- Full cash-play / payout handling
- Deep analytics
- Broad social features
- Multiple advanced contest modes
- Heavy customization beyond the core pool template

## Success Criteria
- Commissioners can launch a pool quickly without support
- Players can join and submit picks with minimal friction
- Live standings and scoring are trusted during tournament windows
- The product is reused across multiple events, not just a one-off test
- Support burden stays low during live play

## Vision
If this wins, it becomes the easiest way to run a private golf pool. From there, it can expand into additional contest formats, paid tiers, and richer social features — but only after it proves it can earn trust in the core commissioner workflow.

## Strategic Positioning
The product should sit between legacy hosted pools and regulated cash-play platforms: simpler than spreadsheets, clearer than old hosts, and less intimidating than sportsbook-like products.
